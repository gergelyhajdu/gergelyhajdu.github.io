Oracle DWH

Oracle DWH development experience at a glance

The following points summarize the strongest work-experience-based elements of my current professional profile.

  • Enterprise data-flow development. Development and maintenance of Oracle database-based data processing, data publication and system-to-system data service processes.
  • Technical design and target structures. Technical design of data flows, creation of target structures aligned with existing data warehouse layers, and adaptation of existing business logic to new data models.
  • Loading logic and process handling. Development of incremental and full reload processes, logic for historical and current-state data, and participation in designing loading chains, organizing them into process flows and supporting their parallel operation.
  • Performance and optimization. Investigation of performance issues in data flows that process large data volumes, and participation in designing and implementing optimization solutions.
  • Validation and troubleshooting. Source-to-target validations, control totals, duplicate and data-difference checks, analysis of failed loads and tracing reporting issues back to source data and loading processes.
DWH details

More detailed DWH responsibilities

Behind the short overview are practical development and collaboration tasks that directly support everyday DWH work.

PL/SQL components

Reading, modifying and adapting existing PL/SQL components, and creating smaller PL/SQL packages, procedures and functions for data processing, server-side file generation and system-to-system data service tasks.

System-to-system data transfer

Development of PL/SQL-based data transfer components within an existing enterprise interface framework: SELECT-based extraction, cursor-based processing, XML data transfer through a shared interface function, and receiving and storing returned acknowledgement data.

Knowledge sharing

Onboarding new and existing colleagues, providing regular professional support, and sharing knowledge during Oracle DWH, SQL and data-flow development tasks.
